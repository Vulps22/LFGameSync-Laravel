Get Started in 3 Simple Steps

Designed to be powerful, yet incredibly simple to use.

Build Your Library

Users add games from Steam, GOG, and more with the simple `/library add` command.

Find Your Crew

Use `/lfg ` to instantly find everyone in the server who owns that specific game.

Play Together

The bot creates a clean post to gather your players and get the game started right away.

Register Any Game

Can't find a game? Use the `/register` command with a store URL. We instantly recognize links from Steam, GOG, and Meta Quest. Other stores are sent for manual approval.

/register url: ...
> Game registered successfully!

/register url: ...
> Store not recognized. Submitted for approval.
GOG and more to come...1
/library add game: ...
> Please select where you own this game...

Build a Cross-Platform Library

Once a game is in the bot's database, any user can add it to their personal library with `/library add`, specifying which store they own it on. This creates a unified view of your community's games, regardless of where they were purchased.

Privacy and Control Included

Your members are always in control. They can use `/sharing on` or `/sharing off` in each server, giving them granular control over where their game library is visible and ensuring their privacy is respected.

/sharing on
> Your library is now visible.

/sharing off
> Your library is now hidden.

Choose Your Crew

The `/lfg` command presents you with a simple dropdown of every member in the server who owns the game and has sharing enabled. You can select specific people or send an open invitation to a role.

The Perfect LFG Post

The final post created by the bot is clean and effective. It tags the chosen players or role, shows the game's image, and includes links to all known store pages, making it easy for everyone to get on the same page.
